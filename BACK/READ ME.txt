platform called " Smart Farm Credit"

1.help farmers get loans based on real estate.
2.connects farmers with buyers and markets.
3.Uses AI tools to give farmers advice.

Break down of Features 
1.User registration(farmer and buyers)
  . farmer sign up with basic info(name , farm location, crops grown)
  
   .buyers(people who want to buy crops)sign up as well

2.Loan application
    
     . Farmer can apply for loans based on their farm performance. we'll use basic data like crops history to approve loans.

3.Market & Product Listings
   
   . farmers can list the crops they want to sell
   .buyers can browse and buy directly from farmers .

4.Basic AI
     
     .a simple chatbot for advice on crop care 
     .no need for complex AI at first just questions and answers based on common problem
     
    
 Tech Stack(basic tools to start with)
1.frontend(app or websites)
     .we'll start with flutter or react native. these are good for both mobile and web apps.
2.Backend (server-side)
    .we can use node.js or Django .these are simple and easy to learn
3.Database
   .we'll use SQLite to store farmer data and products. it's simple and work well for small apps

4.AI

     .we'll use basic rule-based answers.
