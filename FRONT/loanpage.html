Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our loans

How long does loan approval take? +

Most loans are approved within 24-48 hours. Emergency loans can be approved same-day if all documents are in order.

What interest rates do you offer? +

Rates range from 10-15% APR based on loan type and term. Seasonal loans: 12%, Equipment loans: 10%, Emergency loans: 15%.

Can I pay off my loan early? +

Yes! Early repayment is encouraged with no penalties. You'll save on interest by paying early.

What happens if I miss a payment? +

We offer a 7-day grace period. After that, a 2% late fee applies. Contact us immediately if you're having difficulties.

How are funds disbursed? +

Funds are sent via Mobile Money (MTN/Airtel) or direct bank transfer. You'll receive them within 2 hours of approval.
