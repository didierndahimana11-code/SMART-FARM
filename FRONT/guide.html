Essential Farming Tips

Best practices for successful farming in Rwanda

Soil Preparation

Test your soil pH and add organic matter before planting. Rwanda's volcanic soil is rich but may need specific amendments.

Water Management

Use drip irrigation to conserve water. Rwanda has two rainy seasons - plan your planting accordingly.

Pest Control

Use integrated pest management. Rotate crops and use natural predators to control pests without chemicals.

Crop Rotation

Rotate legumes with cereals to naturally replenish soil nitrogen and break pest cycles.
