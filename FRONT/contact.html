Frequently Asked Questions

How can I apply for an agricultural loan?

You can apply for a loan directly through our website on the Loans page. The process involves filling out an application form, providing necessary documentation, and waiting for approval which typically takes 2-3 business days.

What crops do you provide market prices for?

We provide real-time market prices for all major Rwandan crops including beans, maize, potatoes, coffee, tea, rice, and various vegetables. Our data is updated daily from markets across the country.

Do you offer training for new farmers?

Yes, we regularly organize training sessions and workshops for farmers at all experience levels. Check our Events page for upcoming training opportunities, or contact us directly to inquire about specific training needs.

How can I connect with other farmers in my area?

Our Connect platform allows you to find and communicate with other farmers, buyers, and agricultural experts in your region. Simply create an account and use the search filters to find relevant connections.

What should I do if I'm having technical issues with the website?

If you're experiencing technical difficulties, please contact our support team at support@smartfarm.rw or call +250 788 123 456 during business hours. Be sure to describe the issue in detail for faster resolution.
